Biography

After more than 15 years’ experience working with clients individually, she also ran groups and workshops for inner child work, woman's shamanic groups, meditation groups, chakra work, encounter groups, and energy groups.

Philosophy


Cornelia combines the classical coaching approach with a gift of working spiritually and intuitively.

There are times in life when we see no way out, there is no cure and there is no remedy. We battle on and have somehow learned how to survive in this world. We are functioning routinely from day to day, but are neither happy nor fulfilled.

Working with a Spiritual Life Coach at your side, supporting, encouraging, and motivating you on your journey, can help you to identify to making the right choices for your life. You can learn new skills how to improve, change, or simply be with the given situation.

You can experience that the relationship with yourself and ultimately with your surrounding can undergo paradigm shifts and you are able to connect to and view your life in a different light. This can be a very empowering process and experience for you. Your own transformation will come from within yourself.

In my work with people for over 15 years, I have come to realize, that the spiritual aspect is vital for healing. We are not merely flesh nor emotions; we are also spirit and soul. If looked at these dimensions in their entirety, we are able to become integral, wholesome beings and truly healthy on every level. This enables us to fulfill our true life's task or destiny ... or the written plan of the purpose why we are here at this specific time.
